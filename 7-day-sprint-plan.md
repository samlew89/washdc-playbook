# 7-Day Hyper-Density Deployment Sprint Plan

## Sprint Overview

**Objective:** Build a pipeline of 150+ permissioned venues across Dupont Circle and 14th/U St, ready for installer deployment.

**Starting conditions:**
- Zero active deployers/installers
- Hardware available and reserved
- All playbooks, scripts, and systems built
- 2-3 person team available for scouting + follow-up

**End state after 7 days:**
- 400+ raw leads captured
- 150+ venues qualified
- 80+ venues permissioned (verbal yes + site visit scheduled)
- 30+ installs scheduled (pending installer recruitment)
- Installer recruitment pipeline active

```

□ Corner lot?
□ Owner on-site?
□ Spoke to decision-maker?

□ Photo taken?
□ Contact info captured?
```

```
visit. One of our installers will come by for about 10 minutes,
confirm exactly where the units go, and then we'll schedule the
actual install.

What day works best this week? ... And what's the best phone
number to reach you?"
```
