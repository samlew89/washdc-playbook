# Overview

The **DC Playbook** is a hyper-density deployment strategy for Helium Mobile coverage across Washington DC's most vibrant neighborhoods. This playbook provides everything needed to identify, qualify, and onboard 600+ venues across Dupont Circle and the 14th St / U St Corridor.
